HOW TO SET UP YOUR DISCORD WEBHOOK
Receive every lead directly in Discord the moment they submit
=============================================================

STEP 1 — CREATE THE WEBHOOK IN DISCORD
---------------------------------------
1. Open Discord on your computer
2. Go to the server and channel where you want leads
   (e.g. create a channel called #leads or #new-enquiries)
3. Right-click the channel name → Edit Channel
4. Click "Integrations" in the left sidebar
5. Click "Webhooks" → "New Webhook"
6. Give it a name like "Setwise Leads"
7. Click "Copy Webhook URL"
   It will look like: https://discord.com/api/webhooks/123456/abc-xyz
8. Save that URL — you'll need it in Step 2


STEP 2 — ADD TO VERCEL ENVIRONMENT VARIABLES
---------------------------------------------
1. Go to vercel.com and open your project
2. Click "Settings" in the top navigation
3. Click "Environment Variables" in the left sidebar
4. Click "Add New"
5. Fill in:
   Key:   DISCORD_WEBHOOK_URL
   Value: (paste your webhook URL from Step 1)
   Environments: tick Production, Preview, and Development
6. Click "Save"


STEP 3 — REDEPLOY
------------------
1. Go to "Deployments" in your Vercel project
2. Click the three dots (...) on your most recent deployment
3. Click "Redeploy"
4. Wait ~2 minutes for the deployment to complete
5. Done! All leads now go to Discord automatically


WHAT YOU'LL SEE IN DISCORD
---------------------------
Every lead shows as a colour-coded card with:

  🎯 New Lead — [Source Page]   ← title
  👤 Name       ← what they typed
  📧 Email      ← their email address
  📞 Phone      ← phone number (if provided)
  📟 Device     ← printer brand, GPS model, etc.
  🔍 Issue      ← what they need help with
  📍 Source     ← which page/tool they came from

Colour coding per section:
  🔵 Blue     — Printer pages and tools
  🟢 Green    — GPS pages and tools
  🟡 Amber    — Smart Home pages
  🩵 Cyan     — Alexa pages
  🟣 Purple   — Camera pages
  🔴 Red      — Security pages
  🟠 Orange   — Printer troubleshooter tools

TESTING YOUR SETUP
-------------------
After deploying, test it:
1. Go to setwisedigital.com/techbridge/printers
2. Scroll to the "Get Your Free Printer Guide" section
3. Fill in a test name and email
4. Click the button
5. Check your Discord channel — the lead should appear instantly


TROUBLESHOOTING
----------------
Lead not appearing in Discord?
  → Check that DISCORD_WEBHOOK_URL is saved in Vercel environment variables
  → Make sure you redeployed AFTER adding the variable
  → Check that the webhook URL is complete (starts with https://discord.com/api/webhooks/)
  → Try creating a new webhook in Discord (they can expire)

Getting an error on the form?
  → Check Vercel function logs: Vercel project → Functions → view logs
  → Most common cause: webhook URL is wrong or expired
